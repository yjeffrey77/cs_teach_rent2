Process

Formulas

We used the following formulas to calculate the BIPOC values:

Calculate the Number of BIPOC Veteran Teachers \[ \text{(Total Number of Veteran Teachers)} \times \text{(Proportion BIPOC)} \] \[ = \text{Number of BIPOC Veteran Teachers} \]

For example, below is a more precise formula to calculate the number of BIPOC teachers with 10+ years of experience.

\[ \begin{aligned} \text{(Total Number of Teachers with over 10+ years of experience)} \times \text{(Proportion BIPOC)} \\ = \text{Number of BIPOC Teachers with over 10 years of experience} \end{aligned} \]

Calculate Percent BIPOC Veteran Teachers

\[ \frac{\text {Number of BIPOC Veteran Teachers}} {\text {Number of All Teachers}} = \text{Percent of BIPOC Veteran Teachers} \]
